In chess, grandmasters excel not because they are always the smartest individuals in the room, but because they have mastered the patterns in chess that will lead to a checkmate.

The same principle applies to programming. To excel, you must immerse yourself, practice, examine others' code, and recognize patterns that can be applied across various problems you will face.

Strive to become a grandmaster in programming.
